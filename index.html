Guido

Portrayed by Guido Quaroni

Guido is a light blue Alzo/Tutto Forklift from the Cars franchise. Like his best friend and boss Luigi, he is Italian. He is the employee and assistant of Luigi in the Casa Della Tires. Guido is the quickest tire changer in the West, and in the whole world and his life-dream is to change the tires of a real race car, especially if it's a Ferrari. He first debuted in the Pixar film, Cars from 2006 and has been a recurring character in the franchise.

Cars

Like his friend Luigi, Guido is an avid Ferrari racing fan who dreams of performing a real pit stop on a real race car. He practices tire changes at night on a wooden frame he built in his garage. One day, he hopes to set a new world record for the fasted pit stop of all time. But until then, he hangs around the Casa Della Tires in Radiator Springs.

When Lightning McQueen crashed into Radiator Springs, Guido knew his dream would come true. Not a Ferrari, but he was a rookie hotshot that needed a pit crew. When Lightning arrives at the 2006 Piston Cup, he is backed by his new pitcew with Doc Hudson as the leader of the group. At the Piston Cup, Guido is met with attitude from Chick Hicks' pitcrew members. Guido deals with this when he single-handledly changes Lightning McQueen's tires in record time. This was something even accomplished by four experienced pit crewman could do, causing Chick Hick's pitcrew members' mustaches to fall off.

At the end of the movie, Luigi and Guido are stunned to see Michael Schumacher Ferrari come into their store, having been told by Lightning McQueen that they had the best tires in the world. Luigi asked Guido to punch him, before collapsing on his side. Michael Ferrari then addressed Guido in Italian, causing Guido to fall over as well.
